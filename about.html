About Dr. Dhananjay Trivedi

Dr. Dhananjay Trivedi is an Assistant Professor in the Department of Computer Engineering at Gyanmanjari Innovative University, Bhavnagar, Gujarat. He holds a PhD in Philosophy and is qualified in ICPR-JRF and UGC-NET (Philosophy & Hindu Studies). His academic interests lie at the intersection of Indian philosophy, education, consciousness studies, and AI ethics.

Vision for the Journal

The journal is a platform for reclaiming the relevance of philosophy in modern life. It encourages:

  • Interdisciplinary studies linking philosophy with education, science, technology, and public life
  • Honest thought, responsible scholarship, and creative questioning
  • Revivification of ancient wisdom for contemporary use

The journal envisions building a thoughtful, conscious, and sustainable philosophical ecosystem that bridges East and West, tradition and innovation.

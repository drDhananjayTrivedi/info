Welcome to the Official Website

This website presents the academic and editorial work of Dr. Dhananjay Trivedi, Assistant Professor at Gyanmanjari Innovative University, Bhavnagar, Gujarat.

Dr. Trivedi is also the Chief Editor of the India Independent Philosophical Journal, a peer-reviewed publication focused on critical and interdisciplinary philosophical research.
